ABOUT US

Class Leading Workmanship

Hand In Hand With Class Leading Materials

✻

At Aluminium And Glassmaster we use only the best Raw Materials in the construction of products to guarantee products that will withstand the harsh coastal environment. By coupling the best materials with our vast experience, we strive to deliver the best results to our valued clients.

Quality Materials

Our units are manufactured from durable, maintenance-free, high quality corrosion resistant aluminium that will add value to your home, office, store, etc.

Peace Of Mind

25 micron anodising ideal for coastal applications 60-100 micron powder coatings in a spectrum of colours Glazing complies with part "N" glazing of the National Building Regulations

Reliability

All assembly joints are silicone sealed during manufacturing We specialise in the replacement of steel & wooden frames.

Glass

Clear, bronze or grey float glass and mirrors
obscure patterns,
safety glass toughened or laminated,
smart glass,
bullet-resistant and double-glazing.

Aluminium

Windows:
projecting tophung, sidehung, louvres, horizontal & vertical sliding windows.
Doors:
hinge and pivot entrance doors, domestic and heavy-duty sliding doors, sliding folding doors
Other:
shower enclosures, skylights, sunroom enclosures, shopfront, balustrading and custom-made products in a variety of configurations, for various applications.
