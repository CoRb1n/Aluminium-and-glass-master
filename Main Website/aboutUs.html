A Brief Background Of

Aluminium And Glassmaster

♦

Arthur Hodgetts bought Aluminium and Glass Master back in 2003 we are an aluminium and glass company which started from humble beginnings and is now in existence for over 20 years. We are manufacturers and installers of all aluminium products who service the commercial industry as well as the domestic market. Over the years we have grown immensely by giving our customer excellent products and services leaving them satisfied, having them give us future projects. We are based in Margate where all our products are manufactured to our client’s requests by our dedicated and experienced staff. Due to the nature of our business we follow and abide by all building regulations and safety regulations in regards to manufacture and installation of glass and aluminium. Our business aims to continue giving our customers excellent customer satisfaction we specialize in supplying and installing all aluminium products ,we do repairs , free quotations, and consultations.

Aluminium and Glass Master has an employee compliment of 4 qualified fitters and manufactures of aluminium and glass. Our team has over 20 years’ experience and are professional in everything they do. Every job is owner supervised by Arthur to produce a quality installation. We have Petro in our office that will set up appointments and assist our clients with any queries they may have.

Endless Possibilities With Aluminium And Glass

✻

Windows

The aluminium is for our production of the window frames is available in a spectrum of colours. The windows are usually manufactured to a standard configuration, however we are able to customise your window configuration and dimensions.

We specialise in the replacement of steel and wooden frames to aluminium frames and our services also include repairs and servicing to glass and aluminium products.


Enclosures

Maintenance-free structures are manufactured from a combination of glass and aluminium products. The aluminium is available in a spectrum of colours. Enclosures need to be measured on site before quoting. All quotations are free


Glass And Mirrors

Aluminium And Glassmaster is a members of SAGGA (South African Glass & Glazing Association) and all glass supplied and/or glazing carried out is in accordance with their specifications, which in turn conforms with part "N" of the National Building Regulations. Glass supplied includes clear as well as bronze or grey tinted float glass and mirrors, obscure patterned glass, safety glass - laminated or toughened, bullet resistant, acoustic and double glazing. Besides cutting of glass & mirrors, we also offer polishing, beveling and drilling of holes for the mounting of mirrors or for hinge holes in cupboard doors. All Quotations are free


Shower Doors

Maintenance-free aluminium framed showers with safety glass. We offer a large variety of framed and frameless showers - single pivots, double-pivots, bifold, pentagonal, tri-glide, corner-entry, inline doors and the quarter-round shower. We can also customise shower enclosures to suit your needs. All Quotations are free


Sliding Doors

Hinge and pivot entrance doors, domestic and heavy-duty sliding doors, sliding folding doors. The aluminium is available in a spectrum of colours. The doors are often personalised by adding additional aluminium, different handles and/or sandblasting. We specialise in the replacement of steel and wooden frames to aluminium frames and our services also include repairs and servicing to glass and aluminium. All quotations are free.


Balustrades

Maintenance-free structures are manufactured from a combination of glass and aluminium products. The aluminium is available in a spectrum of colours. Sandblasted designs on the glass panels are sometimes used to great effect. All quotations are free.
